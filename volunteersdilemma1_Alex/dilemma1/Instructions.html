In your group, each member has received 1 pound bonus on top of the participation fee of 1.50. All group members will receive this bonus if at least one person chooses to complete a task.

The task will take about [insert timeframe] and will have to be completed alone. Any one of you can volunteer to do the task. If at least one person does, then everyone receives their bonus. If no one does, then none of the group members receive the bonus. You can volunteer to complete this task yourself or you can choose to go straight to the end questionnaire. You will not know what the other participants' choices are, each of you chooses independently. After all group members have made their decisions, you will find out whether you have received the bonus or not.


Backwards-typing task

The task you can choose to complete is a backwards-typing task. In this backwards typing task, you will be shown a series of random strings. Each of these strings consists of 10 signs (= numbers and letters).

An example string is: %rJ?Q9PvX8

Your goal is to type the strings over backwards, precisely in reverse order. So, if the displayed string is %rJ?Q9PvX8, you should type: 8XvP9Q?Jr%. If you reverse 10 strings correctly, you are allowed to proceed.

This task is not mandatory. It is your choice whether you perform it or not for the benefit of the group. If you choose to skip it, you will be taken to the end questionnaire.

You can also opt out during the task. If you choose to opt out before finishing all 10 strings, the task will be considered not completed and you will be taken to the end questionnaire.
